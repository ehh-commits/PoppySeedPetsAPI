Poppyopedia » Glossary » Groups

Every pet can be a small number of groups. When groups meet, they perform some activity based on the type of group.

Astronomy Lab

When an astronomy lab meets, its members will explore the cosmos, and earn experience in the process!

Astronomers can discover (and acquire) objects from space. (How exactly the pets actually get the stuff, I leave to your imagination...)

Band

When a band meets, its members will work on producing an album, and earn experience by doing so!

When an album is complete, all members of the band receive a copy. Pets with greater musical skill will produce larger albums.

Gaming Group

Pets in gaming groups occasionally meet to play games, including video games, table-top role-playing games, board games, and more.

A variety of the pets' skills are tested, depending on the game played, and pets will occasionally come home with dice, and other gaming equipment.

Sportsball Team

Sportsball teams meet to play... Sportsball! And earn experience by doing so!

Players may occasionally return home with Sportsball equipment...

Gardening Club

Gardening clubs meet to grow plants together, and earn experience as they do!

Once their harvest is all grown up, all members will recieve the produce. Pets with higher nature skill will have bigger harvests.
